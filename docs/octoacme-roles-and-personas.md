# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers craft user experiences and visual designs that are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs. They ensure that product interfaces are both functional and aesthetically appealing, collaborating closely with product and engineering teams.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Define design systems and maintain visual consistency
- Collaborate with Product Managers to understand user requirements
- Provide design specifications and assets to developers
- Participate in design reviews and iterate based on feedback
- Ensure accessibility and responsive design standards

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered designs that meet business objectives
- Improve user satisfaction and reduce friction in user journeys
- Maintain design consistency across products and platforms
- Balance aesthetic quality with technical feasibility

### Typical Communication
- Design critiques and review sessions
- Collaboration sessions with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings presentations
- Design specs and handoff documentation

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Align on user needs, feature priorities, and success metrics
- **Developers**: Provide design specifications, clarify implementation details, and validate delivered features
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate design timelines, dependencies, and milestone reviews
- **Customer Support Lead**: Gather user feedback and pain points to inform design improvements

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts transform data into actionable insights that drive informed decision-making. They support product and project teams by analyzing trends, validating metrics, and providing evidence-based recommendations.

### Responsibilities
- Collect, clean, and analyze data from multiple sources
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholder visibility
- Validate success metrics and KPIs
- Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in data
- Provide recommendations based on data analysis
- Support A/B testing and experiment design
- Maintain data quality and documentation

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision-making across teams
- Provide timely and accurate insights for product and project decisions
- Improve visibility into product performance and user behavior
- Identify opportunities for optimization and growth

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews with Product Managers
- Data insights presentations to stakeholders
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and findings
- Documentation of data sources and methodologies

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Provide metrics analysis to inform roadmap priorities and feature validation
- **Project Managers**: Supply project performance data and trend analysis for status reporting
- **Developers**: Collaborate on instrumentation, logging, and data collection implementation
- **Business Analyst**: Share insights to validate requirements and business case assumptions

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads oversee application and data security, ensuring that security best practices are integrated throughout the development lifecycle. They protect systems, data, and users from security threats and vulnerabilities.

### Responsibilities
- Define and enforce security standards and policies
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Oversee vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
- Implement security controls in CI/CD pipelines
- Lead incident response and coordinate remediation efforts
- Provide security training and guidance to teams
- Ensure compliance with security regulations and standards

### Goals
- Minimize security risks and vulnerabilities
- Build security into the development process
- Respond quickly and effectively to security incidents
- Foster a security-aware culture across teams

### Typical Communication
- Security review sessions during planning and design
- Incident response communications and post-mortems
- Security training and awareness sessions
- Compliance and audit reports

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Review code for security issues, provide secure coding guidance, and validate fixes
- **QA/Testing**: Coordinate security testing and vulnerability scanning
- **Project Managers**: Identify security-related risks and ensure adequate time for security activities
- **Release Manager**: Ensure security validations are complete before deployments

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads represent end-user needs and feedback within the product development process. They ensure that customer voices are heard and that products meet user expectations while supporting smooth feature releases.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and synthesize customer feedback and pain points
- Support release readiness and customer communications
- Create and maintain customer-facing documentation
- Coordinate training for support teams on new features
- Escalate critical customer issues to product and engineering
- Track and report on common support patterns and trends
- Ensure smooth handoff of new features to support teams

### Goals
- Improve customer satisfaction and reduce support burden
- Ensure customer needs inform product decisions
- Enable efficient issue resolution and escalation
- Facilitate clear communication of product changes to users

### Typical Communication
- Weekly syncs with Product Managers on customer feedback
- Release readiness meetings and feature briefings
- Customer communication drafts and documentation
- Escalation reports and critical issue notifications

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Share customer insights, pain points, and feature requests to inform priorities
- **Developers**: Escalate technical issues and clarify behavior for troubleshooting
- **UX/UI Designer**: Provide user feedback on design and usability issues
- **Release Manager**: Coordinate on release communications, documentation, and training materials

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate release timelines, deployment processes, and rollback plans. They ensure that software releases are executed smoothly, with minimal disruption to users and clear communication to all stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and coordinate release schedules and windows
- Manage deployment processes and automation
- Coordinate release readiness reviews and go/no-go decisions
- Prepare and maintain rollback and contingency plans
- Track and communicate release status to stakeholders
- Facilitate post-release validation and monitoring
- Maintain release documentation and runbooks

### Goals
- Execute zero-downtime, reliable deployments
- Minimize deployment risks and failures
- Ensure clear communication around releases
- Continuously improve release processes and automation

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with engineering and product
- Go/no-go decision meetings with stakeholders
- Deployment status updates and incident communications
- Post-release reports and retrospectives

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Coordinate code freeze dates, deployment readiness, and technical requirements
- **QA/Testing**: Ensure testing is complete and sign-off is obtained before release
- **Project Managers**: Align release schedules with project timelines and dependencies
- **Security Lead**: Validate security checks and approvals are complete before deployment
- **Customer Support Lead**: Coordinate release communications and ensure support readiness

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts clarify requirements and translate business needs into actionable tasks for technical teams. They serve as a bridge between stakeholders, product teams, and engineering to ensure shared understanding and alignment.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into functional specifications
- Create process flows, use cases, and user stories
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business objectives
- Identify process improvements and optimization opportunities
- Support acceptance testing and solution validation

### Goals
- Ensure clear, actionable requirements that minimize rework
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams
- Validate that solutions deliver intended business value
- Improve process efficiency and effectiveness

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- User story refinement sessions with development teams
- Business case presentations and feasibility analysis
- Acceptance criteria documentation and validation sessions

### Collaboration with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on requirements definition and ensure alignment with product strategy
- **Developers**: Clarify requirements, answer questions, and validate technical approaches
- **Project Managers**: Support planning with requirements clarity, scope definition, and risk identification
- **Data Analyst**: Validate business metrics and success criteria with data insights
- **UX/UI Designer**: Ensure user experience aligns with business requirements and processes
